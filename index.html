Our Popular Dishes

Indian cuisine is known for its rich flavors, spices, and variety. At our restaurant,we proudly serve authentic Indian dishes that represent different regions of India.Each dish is prepared with traditional spices and fresh ingredients to give you the true taste of India.

  • Veg Biryani: A flavorful rice dish cooked with fresh vegetables, aromatic spices, and herbs.
  • Chicken Biryani: Long-grain basmati rice cooked with tender chicken and traditional spices.
  • Paneer Butter Masala: Soft paneer cubes cooked in a creamy tomato-based gravy.
  • Grilled Chicken: Juicy chicken marinated with spices and grilled to perfection.
  • Veg Burger: Crispy vegetable patty served with fresh lettuce and sauces.
  • Gulab Jamun: Soft milk-based sweet soaked in sugar syrup.
